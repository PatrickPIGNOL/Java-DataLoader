\doxysubsection{Description détaillée}
The Jaro{\ucr}\+Winkler distance metric is designed and best suited for short strings such as person names, and to detect typos; it is (roughly) a variation of Damerau-\/\+Levenshtein, where the substitution of 2 close characters is considered less important then the substitution of 2 characters that a far from each other. 

Jaro-\/\+Winkler was developed in the area of record linkage (duplicate detection) (Winkler, 1990). It returns a value in the interval \mbox{[}0.\+0, 1.\+0\mbox{]}. The distance is computed as 1 -\/ Jaro-\/\+Winkler similarity. \begin{DoxyAuthor}{Auteur}
