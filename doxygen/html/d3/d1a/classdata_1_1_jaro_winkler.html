Description d√©taill√©e

The JaroÔøΩWinkler distance metric is designed and best suited for short strings such as person names, and to detect typos; it is (roughly) a variation of Damerau-Levenshtein, where the substitution of 2 close characters is considered less important then the substitution of 2 characters that a far from each other.

Jaro-Winkler was developed in the area of record linkage (duplicate detection) (Winkler, 1990). It returns a value in the interval [0.0, 1.0]. The distance is computed as 1 - Jaro-Winkler similarity.
